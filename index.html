    AI, Agents, Analytics, Apps, Data and Architecture

    Practical thinking for Prosumers building better decisions with AI, Agents, Analytics, Apps, and Data.

    Common Acumen is a personal technology journal focused on AI, Agents, Analytics, Apps, Data platforms, and Cloud architecture. The goal is simple: make hard ideas clearer, more useful, and easier to put into practice. The new assumption is Prosumption because in modern digital, frontier firms and homes the default expectation is no longer that people are passive consumers. Instead, they are using cloud-based frontier and local AI models and AI Coding Agents to simultaneously produce (create, design, and distribute) Agents, Apps, Analytics, and Data products that they and others consume.
