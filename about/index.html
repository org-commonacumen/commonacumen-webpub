    About

    Common Acumen a personal technology journal targeted at the Prosumers and focused on:

    • AI
    • Agents
    • Analytics
    • Apps
    • Data platforms
    • Cloud architecture
    • cloud-based frontier models
    • local AI models and inferencing
    • AI Coding Agents

    The new assumption is Prosumption because in modern digital, frontier firms and homes the default expectation is no longer that people are passive consumers. Instead, they are using cloud-based frontier and local AI models and AI Coding Agents to simultaneously create, design, and distribute AI, Agents, Apps, Analytics, and Data products that they and others consume.

    What is a Prosumer?

    Prosumers are people that simultaneously produce (create, design, and distribute) and consume AI, Agents, Apps, Analytics, and Data products.
